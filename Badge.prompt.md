Small uppercase label or pill — used for case-study meta lines and category chips.

```jsx
<Badge>Cliente · Barbería</Badge>
<Badge variant="solid">Nuevo</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline">Marca + Sitio</Badge>
```

Variants: `meta` (gold uppercase eyebrow, no background — default), `solid` (filled gold pill), `outline` (gold-tinted bordered pill), `neutral` (white bordered pill). All Inter, 600 weight.
