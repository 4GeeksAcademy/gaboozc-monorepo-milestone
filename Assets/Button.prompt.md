A gold call-to-action button with the brand's signature lift-on-hover; use for primary actions, outline secondaries, and low-emphasis ghosts.

```jsx
<Button href="/contacto">Quiero existir en internet</Button>
<Button variant="secondary">Ver cómo lo hacemos</Button>
<Button variant="ghost" size="sm">Saber más →</Button>
```

Variants: `primary` (solid gold, default), `secondary` (outline that tints gold on hover), `ghost` (text-only gold). Sizes: `sm`, `md` (default), `lg`. Pass `href` to render as a link, or `onClick` for a button. Hover lifts −2px with a gold glow; active resets. Always Inter, 600 weight.
